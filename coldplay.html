Coldplay

Coldplay is one of the biggest bands for their time, back in the 2000's they made hit song after hit song, and that didn't change one bit in 2021 when they announched their new album Music of the Spheres.

listen to Music of the Spheres on Spotify!

The setlist of Music of the Spheres excistst of 25 songs in total, wich include some old clasic songs.

Music of the Spheres World Tour, Amsterdam Johan Cruiff Arena 2023 wristband project.

For this concert there was a fanproject during the whole concert where fans were giving a wrist band which had a light reacting to all songs so they made little shapes and stars. Every show has these light bands which i find super cute and creative.

About the venue

The venue where Music of the Spheres tour took place was Johan Cruijff Arena (Johan cruijff Boulevard 1,1101 AX Amsterdam) in Amsterdam, this venue excists of a standing pit with a capacity of 55.885 people and two rings of seating tickets. You can get to the venue by train (Bijlmer Arena) then it's a short 5 minute walk to the venue itself. There is also a parking space by the arena for which you need to buy an extra parking ticket besides your concert ticket.
